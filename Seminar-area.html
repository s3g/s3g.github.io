Seminar area

Seminar area

The seminar area of the studio is for flexible use and supports audio-visual presentations independent from the 25.4 array.

Equipment includes:

    • Large-format HDMI video display, wall mounted
    • 2 Genelec 8050B studio monitors
    • Crestron AV switcher
    • Balanced, line-level audio inputs (2 XLR for direct connection from audio interfaces)
    • Moveable tables and chairs
